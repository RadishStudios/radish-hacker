Where Product Is Sold
FragranceDTC + Retail+

The Magnolia Partnership

We’d love Magnolia to participate in supporting the merchandising strategy. We have a few ideas but are open to everything.

Merchandise Pre-BuyMagnolia+
Magnolia commits to a pre-buy at full retail before launch, 80 perfumes at $125 and 50 Baby-Ts at $100, totaling $15,000, and uses that product to seed press, influencers, tastemakers, and editors. This de-risks production, counts toward investor recoup from unit one, and generates organic visibility at the same time.
Shared PR + Niche PlacementMagnolia+
Open their network and collaborate on a shared PR strategy. The ask is to identify where product fits naturally within their existing plan, Substack writers, niche editorial, tastemaker communities, and potentially allocate a small dedicated budget toward culturally credible placements.
Product in Press MaterialsMagnolia+
Place the fragrance, Baby-Ts, or necklace within existing film shoots, press kits, behind-the-scenes content, or social assets. Organic visibility at no extra production cost.
